One of the best ways to combat climate change is for businesses to replant trees and reforest the forests. By replanting every tree we chop down we can stop or at leats postpone many of the consequences that will come from deforestation.

Another way businesses can combat the effects of deforestation is with ecoforestry. This is the process of carefully selecting trees to chop down in order to cause the least possible damage to the surrounding are and species.
